Meet the Creator

Author Elder thinks outside of the box and is intrinsic in the way he thinks and creates stories through "visual gore" and future adaptations on the human civilization. The stories being created so masterfully have been carefully selected from an array of possible outcomes. There is a marketed genre he intends to aim for -- He has always had a love for Sci-Fi & more recently Horror. There aren't many authors who combine both of these genres together but he hopes one day he will be known as the godfather of "Sci-Gore"
